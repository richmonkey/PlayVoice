How to Use GameVoice

🔑 1. Signing In

Step 1 — Open GameVoice. On the sign-in screen, tap Continue with Google or the Sign in with Apple button.
Step 2 — Complete the Google or Apple authentication flow. No additional registration is required.
Step 3 — On your first sign-in, GameVoice automatically creates your personal voice channel. You'll be taken directly to the Home screen.
💡 Your login is persistent — you won't need to sign in again unless you explicitly sign out.

📻 2. Entering a Voice Room

Step 1 — On the Home screen, tap your My Channel card at the top to enter your own voice room, or tap any channel in the Followed Channels list.
Step 2 — The Voice Room screen opens and connects automatically. Grant microphone permission when prompted.
Step 3 — All online members in the room are displayed. Your voice is transmitted in real time to everyone in the room.
Step 4 — Use the control bar at the bottom to: Mute/Unmute your microphone, toggle Speaker/Earpiece, or Leave the room.
💡 Microphone is unmuted by default when you join. Tap Mute to silence yourself.

🔍 3. Searching & Following Users

Step 1 — Tap the Search button in the top bar on the Home screen.
Step 2 — Type a player's name or channel name. Results appear as you type.
Step 3 — Tap Follow next to a user. Their channel is immediately added to your Home screen's Followed Channels list.
Step 4 — To unfollow, tap Following on the same user — it toggles back to Follow.

⚙️ 4. Profile & Channel Name

Step 1 — Tap the person icon in the top-right corner of the Home screen to open your Profile.
Step 2 — Tap Channel Name to edit your channel's display name (2–30 characters).
Step 3 — Tap Save. The new name is reflected on your channel card immediately.

🌗 5. Appearance (Light / Dark / System)

Step 1 — Tap the gear icon (Settings) in the top-right of the Home screen.
Step 2 — Tap Appearance and choose Light, Dark, or Follow System.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app ask for microphone permission?

GameVoice uses your microphone exclusively to transmit your voice in real-time to other users in the same voice room. Audio is never recorded or stored. You can revoke microphone access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → GameVoice.

Can I use GameVoice without a Google or Apple account?

No. GameVoice requires a Google or Apple account to create your user identity and personal voice channel. This ensures a trusted environment and prevents anonymous abuse.

How many people can join a voice room?

There is no strict limit on the number of participants in a voice room. However, for the best audio quality, rooms with fewer than 20 concurrent speakers are recommended.

I can't hear others / others can't hear me. What do I do?

Try these steps in order:

  1. Check that microphone permission is granted: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → GameVoice.
  2. Make sure you are not muted (tap Unmute in the control bar).
  3. Check your Speaker/Earpiece toggle — try switching modes.
  4. Leave and rejoin the voice room.
  5. Force-quit the app and relaunch.

If the problem persists, email us at daibou007@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.

Why did my followed channels disappear?

Followed channels are stored on the server and linked to your account. If they are missing, try pulling down to refresh the Home screen. If the issue persists, sign out and sign back in.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion and removal of all associated data, please email daibou007@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

The app crashed or something isn't working.

Force-quit and relaunch the app. If the problem persists, email us at daibou007@gmail.com with a description of what happened, your device model, and iOS version.
