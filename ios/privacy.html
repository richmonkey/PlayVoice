Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

Summary: GameVoice collects only the minimum information required to provide voice chat services. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. Your audio is never recorded or stored.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GameVoice ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application on iOS. GameVoice is a real-time voice chat application designed for gaming squads, allowing users to create personal voice channels and communicate with others.

By using GameVoice, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

GameVoice collects limited information necessary to provide its core services:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo URL from the identity provider. This information is used solely to create and display your in-app identity.
  • Channel & Follow Data: Your personal channel name and the list of users you follow are stored on our servers to power the app's core features.
  • Voice Communication: Real-time audio is transmitted over encrypted WebRTC connections. Audio is never recorded, stored, or analyzed by us.

We do not collect:

  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

3. Microphone Permission

GameVoice requires access to your device microphone to enable real-time voice chat. The microphone is activated only when you are actively inside a voice room. Audio is transmitted directly to other participants over an encrypted WebRTC connection and is never saved to any server.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → GameVoice. Revoking permission will disable voice chat functionality.

4. Google & Apple Sign-In

GameVoice uses Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In as the exclusive authentication methods. We do not store your Google or Apple password. Upon sign-in, we receive a secure identity token which is verified server-side. We store your display name, email address, and avatar URL to display your profile within the app.

Apple Sign-In users may choose to hide their email address; the app will function normally with Apple's private relay address.

5. Data Storage & Security

Account information (display name, email, channel name, follow relationships) is stored on our backend servers with industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We retain this data for as long as your account is active.

To request deletion of your data, please contact us at daibou007@gmail.com.

6. Third-Party Services

GameVoice integrates the following third-party authentication services:

  • Google Sign-In — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In — governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

GameVoice is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at daibou007@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
